    Task 8: Funding Report

    Report on the Funding Options for Escape to Hong Kong Island

    The following is a report on the two funding options available to our new game Escape to Hong Kong Island.

    To start with, the first funding option we can consider is Gamezstart, a well-known funding website for board games. By using Gamezstart, we get to advertise our game and attract investors from all over the world as it is a global website that allows broad game developers to raise money from anywhere in the world. Another advantage of Gamezstart is the flexibility it offers as we can set the target needed produce our game. However, one drawback of using Gamezstart is that our game will be ignored if at the same time another big company is raising fund for their project on Gamezstart. Also, we may have to abandon the project if we fail to reach the target. Moreover, international shipping can be expensive for board games when we have to ship our broad games to our investors.

    Another funding option available for us is that Acron Grant, which is sponsored by Melaka Bank. We can receive HK$ 250, 000 as a one-time award if we win the grant. Moreover, Acron Grant will provide us with financial and advertising advice for a year after we win the grant. Nevertheless, it is very difficult to win the grant since 287 companies applied last year and there was only one winner. More importantly, we value our independence and we do not want others to control us, but Acron Grant is known for wanting to control everytiing.

    Based the advantages and disadvantages for the two funding options mentioned above, we can consider them carefully and then proceed to make the final decision for the funding options we would like to choose to fund our game.

    Task 9: Email to Cafe Owner

    Subject: Re: Booking Tables for Competition

    Dear Mr Shim,

    Thank you for your email, and now I’m writing to answer your questions and provide more details about the booking for the competition at Crossroads Game Cafe.

    First of all, we would like to book all eight tables for the 32 people coming to the competition. On top of that, we would like to make the booking for 2 May, Wednesday from 6 pm to 9 pm. We will pay the fee for all the customers, so we would kindly ask you for a discount of 50%.

    In addition, we would appreciate that if you can contact your regular customers about the competition, and it would be great if you can inform your customers that Eric Park will come as a special guest and see if they would like to participate in the competition.

    These are all the details that we can provide you at the moment. If you have any more questions, please don’t hesitate to contact as again. Again, thank you for your time and attention, and we look forward to holding our competition at Crossroads Game Cafe.

    Best regards,
    Sammy Tsang
    Griffin Workshop

    Task 10: Email to Artist

    Subject: Feedback on the Artwrok for Escape to Hong Kong Isalnd

    Dear Mr. Au,

    Thank you for creating the artwork for Escape to Hong Kong Island, and we really appreciate your effort and hard work. In this email, I am writing to provide you some feedback on the art work.

    To start with, regarding the artwork on the map, we received a lot of positive response from the majority of the teenagers and parents based on a survey we conducted, and both teenagers and parents loved the artwork on the map.

    In terms of the art on the cards, most of the teenagers loved it, but the majority of parents had a negative response to that since they thought it has too much blood. Usually, it’s the parents who have the final say in whether the game will be bought, so parents will not buy the game if the art work it is too graphic or violent.

    With regard to the text on the map, unfortunately majority of both parents and teenagers said that it wasn’t really easy to read the text on the map, and they thought that the colour of the text needs to be darker.

    In conclusion, based on the feedback above, could you please make the changes to the text on the map as well as the artwork on the cards? We would really appreciate that if you can complete all the changes by 27th April. Again, thank you for creating the artwork for us, and we look forward to seein the updated versions of your work.

    Regards,
    Sammy Tsang
    Griffin Wrokshop
