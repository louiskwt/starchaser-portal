## Task 6: Email

Subject: Questions and Requests for Changes to the Bird Watching Tour

Dear Mr. Chan,

As always, we thank you for working with our company to hold the Bird Watching Tour in the Hong Kong Botanical Gardens. In this email, I am writing to ask you some questions regarding the trip and inform you a change we are making to the Bird Watching Tour.

To start with, I want to talk about the change we are making to the Bird Watching Tour and provide you the reasons behind the change. We have decided to change the start time from 7:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. for the Bird Watching Tour because not enough people are signing up tour and we learned form the feedback from the participants that the tour starts too early. As a result, the start time of the Bird Watching Tour has been changed to 9:30 a.m. instead, and we hope you can understand why we make this change.

In terms of the questions about the tour, we wonder if it is possible for the staff to give out free refreshments such as ice cream because the weather in Hong Kong has been extremely hot recently and the participants will have to walk for a long time during the tour. Moreover, we would like to know if it is possible to refund the participants if there are not sufficient bird sightings or there is poor weather during the tour. We have this question because some who joined the tour previously have left us negative reviews due to insufficient bird sightings and poor wether condition. Although we provided them with coupons and discounts for future tours, the participants were still not happy about it as they believed the discounts and coupons would not make up for the time they lost and they thought only a refund would be suitable.

These are the all the questions and information about the change we are making to the Bird Watching Tour, and we look forward to hearing from you. Again, thank you for working closely with us to provide the Bird Watching Tour.

Best regards, </br>
Nicole Lin</br>
Secretary at World City Travel</br>
