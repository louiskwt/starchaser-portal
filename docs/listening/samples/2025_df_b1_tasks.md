To: Frank Brown<br/>
From: Nico Lin<br/>
Subject: Invitation to be the speaker at the Opening Event of the Asian Games Week

Dear Mr. Brown,

As the president of the Healthy Living Club at Ballater High School, I am writing to invite you to be the guest speaker at the Opening Event of the Asian Games Week at our school.

First of all, the details of the event are as follows. The event is on 13th October from 2:00 pm to 3:15pm at the main hall, and the speech topic is going to be about why ultimate frisbee should be added to the Asian Games. After the speech, we will have a discussion about your sport, and there will be questions from students as our studnets wanted to learn more about ultimate frisbee.

Ultimate Frisbee was chosen to be the focus on this speech for the opening event because this sport got the highest number of votes in a survey conducted at our school. Also, our students think ultimate frisbee is fun to play and exciting to watch.

To show that we value your time and effort to be our speaker, we can offer you a payment of 1000 dollars for this event. We sincerely hope that you will accept this invitation, and please contact me if you have any more questions about the arrangement for being the speaker at the Opening Event of the Asian Games Week. We look forward to hearing from you. Thank you for your attention.

Regards,<br/>
Nico Lin<br/>
Preisdent of Healthy Living Club
