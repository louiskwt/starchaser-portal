# DSE 2024 Paper 2 Part A: Complaint Email

Dear Hotel Manager of Seaside Hotel,

Recently, I booked a one-night stay at the Seaside Hotel for my summer staycation. Unfortunately, I was utterly dissatisfied with my experience at your hotel because the room I stayed in was drastically different from what was shown on your official website. In this email, I am writing to explain why I was disappointed and what I would like you to do.

To begin with, I had high expectations of the balcony and ocean view, but upon my arrival, I discovered that the ocean view was partially blocked by a new apartment building which was under construction. Moreover, due to the ongoing construction work, I dared not step outside onto the dust-filled balcony or let the noise from the construction disrupt my supposedly peaceful staycation.

Besides that, the wifi and air conditioning were not functioning properly. According to the website, the room I booked should have provided wifi connection and air conditioning. However, the wifi signal was very poor, and the air conditioning was very weak. Without being able to enjoy the balcony and ocean view, I thought I could at least spend the night watching Netflix and relaxing in a cool room. But sadly, I could hardly watch anything with the abysmal wifi connection while being stuck in a room that was too hot for me to sleep in.

As a matter of fact, I tried resolving this matter with your staff, and although they were very sorry about the room, they refused to provide me with another room, claiming that the hotel was fully booked that night. Instead, they offered me extra cool drinks through room service. Nevertheless, they later charged me extra money for the drinks, which I thought should have been free and included in the room service as advertised on the website.

Given all these unpleasant experiences, I believe that your hotel has failed to provide the quality of service and accommodation that was promised. Because of that, I would like your hotel to give me a full refund for my stay, including the extra charges for the drinks. In addition, I strongly suggest that you update the photos and descriptions on your official website so that future guests will not be misled by outdated or inaccurate information. It would also be appreciated if your hotel could improve the maintenance of its facilities, especially the wifi and air conditioning, in order to ensure a more comfortable stay for customers.

I hope you will take my complaint seriously and handle this matter promptly, and I look forward to receiving your reply and a reasonable explanation as soon as possible.

Yours faithfully,<br/>
Chris Wong
